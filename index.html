Food game: Like or Dislike!

Let's play a food game. U will see random images of food. If you like the food you see, u can give it a like with your hand. If you dislike the food, u can give it a dislike with your hand. But first, u need to train the computer. Show your best like or dislike gesture to the camera and press the like or dislike button. After you have trained the computer with 10+ images, you can start playing!
